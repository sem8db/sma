Octoparse

Step 1: Open Octoparse App
Step 2: In Octoparse Home Page , Go to new task and select Task Template.
Step 3: Here we can find a list of task template to choose from. We have selected the YouTube Video List
Scrapper
Step 4: Enter links/keywords you want to search and scroll times, higher the number of scroll times more data will
be collected, also it will take more time. Then Click on Start Button.
Step 5: Select Standard Mode in the Run on your device option
Step 6: Wait for a few seconds to complete data extraction. Then we can view our data.
Step 7: Task Stopped
